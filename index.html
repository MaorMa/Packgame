Developers: Maor Maimon | Yaniv Knobel

Hello there! We are students at the department of Software and information systems engineering at ben gurion university of the negev.

This pacman game was developed as part of university course "Internet Programming Environments"

jQuery Plugins take place during this game: During coding login & register forms, We used jQuery like ($("#id") to get forms data from the user and also $(document).ready to get DOM after page was loaded.

Difficulties we faced during game development: We had some problems during drawing the monsters from images (not working at the beginning), and in general during game coding.

In general, we enjoyed to create this awesome game, and and we're glad we've learned more about js and jQuery in particularly.
